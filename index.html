About Me

Hi, I'm a passionate and dedicated web developer with a keen eye for detail and a drive for continuous learning. With a background in Genetics and Human Physiology, I've seamlessly transitioned into the world of technology, bringing a unique perspective and problem-solving approach to my projects.

I specialize in creating intuitive and visually appealing websites and applications. My expertise spans both front-end and back-end development, including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and various frameworks like React and Node.js. I'm particularly enthusiastic about UI/UX design and strive to make user experiences both functional and delightful.

When I'm not coding, you can find me engaging in digital literacy initiatives, teaching teenagers in South Africa the skills they need to thrive in the tech world. I believe in the power of technology to make a positive impact and am committed to using my skills to contribute to meaningful projects and communities.

Whether you're looking for a developer to bring your ideas to life or simply want to connect and chat about technology and design, feel free to reach out. Let's build something amazing together!
